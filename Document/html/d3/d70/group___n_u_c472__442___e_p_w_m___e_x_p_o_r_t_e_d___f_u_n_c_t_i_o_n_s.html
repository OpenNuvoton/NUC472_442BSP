  • EPWM_BRK0_BKP0
  • EPWM_BRK0_CPO0
  • EPWM_BRK0_CPO1
  • EPWM_BRK0_CPO2
  • EPWM_BRK1_LVDBKEN
  • EPWM_BK1SEL_BKP1
  • EPWM_BK1SEL_CPO0

  • EPWM_BK1SEL_CPO2
